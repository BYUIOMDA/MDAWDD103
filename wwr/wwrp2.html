A Better Get-away

Is white-water rafting safe?
At WWR we take safety seriously. We want our riders to enjoy a unique experience while cruising down the river. We brief our riders before the trip on safety techniques. Our staff are kept up to a high company standard of training for any situation along the river.

What is required to take ride on the rapids?
A sense of adventure! We have a variety of circuits for any level of comfort.

What can we expect for overnight trips?
We bring tents and full campsite equipment. We require riders to bring their own sleeping bag and pad. Cots can be rented for an additional fee. Included in our overnight trip rate, we have dinner and breakfast options. Please notify staff beforehand regarding any food allergies.

What items should/shouldn't I bring?
We encourage riders to bring things like sunproof hats, glasses, sunscreen, etc. DO NOT BRING anything you don't want to lose to the river or the wild.

What if I need to cancel my reservation?
We can refund the full total up to 2 weeks before the trip and we offer a prorated refund up to 12 hours before the ride.

What will happen in the lower excitement moments of the trip?
We want customers to enjoy every minute of the journey. We keep the fun rolling with games, stories, jokes, and all kinds of fun.
