Wireframes

Wireframes

Home

Until the webpage expands, the nav bar will be at the top but split by the Utah Beach logo. The description below should be a brief explanation of what we do. The black image below will be our mission statement and a reflection by outside endorsement of what we have accomplished.

About Us

This wireframe demonstates what who the founder of Utah Beach is and what he aims to accomplish. We can add anyone to this as consultants or something akin. At the request of the owner, we can add our partners and any affiliate beneath frontpage leadership. This frontpage leadership would obviously center around the founder.

Contact Us

This page will show the coverage map for UtahBeach. It will also include links, contact information, and processes for getting in contact. The footer displays only two social media icons. If the owner wants to focus on just two media avenues, this give him an idea of what it may look like.
